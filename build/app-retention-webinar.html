Apps Retention: Benchmarks, Strategies And Best Practices

In a hyper-competitive app space, marketers are forced to shift their attention from acquisition to retention. With churn reaching alarmingly high rates, the ability to hold on to your users is at the core of app success. Without users sticking around, proper monetization is all but impossible.

How can marketers find the right users and get them to come back again, and again, and again? Himanshu Dilip Kulkarni, Director of Sales at AppsFlyer and Ashwin S.L. Head of Marketing at MoEngage, unlock the best practices on how you can drive retention and be in the position to sustain commercial success.

YOU'LL LEARN:

  • ⦿ WHY: An in-depth understanding of how retention rates are affected by various processes.

  • ⦿ ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS: Data-backed insights, and sector-wise breakdowns of retention rates from e-commerce, travel, and OTT. Cohort analysis.

  • ⦿ HOW: Leverage learnings across multiple industries for your customer engagement initiatives.
